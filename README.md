# Online Retail Sales Analysis

## Overview
End-to-end data analysis project analyzing online retail transactions to identify revenue drivers, customer behavior, sales trends, and retention patterns.

## Business Questions
- Which products drive the most revenue?
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- How do sales trend over time?
- Are customers returning?

## Tools Used
- Excel (Power Query)
- PostgreSQL
- Tableau

## Key Insights
- Revenue is highly concentrated among a small number of products.
- A small segment of customers contributes disproportionately to total revenue.
- Sales show strong seasonal patterns.
- ~72% of customers are repeat buyers.
